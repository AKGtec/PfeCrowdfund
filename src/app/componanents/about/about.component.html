Our Story

CrowdFund was founded in 2023 with a simple idea: to create a platform where anyone could turn their creative vision into reality. What started as a small team of passionate individuals has grown into a thriving community of creators and backers.

Today, we've helped fund over 1,000 projects across various categories including technology, art, social causes, and entrepreneurship. Our platform has become a launchpad for innovative ideas and a bridge between creators and supporters.
